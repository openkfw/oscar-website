OSCAR
Better data. Better decisions. Better lives.

In humanitarian crises, we often know too little, too late. Without such knowledge, humanitarian resources are frequently not allocated to those who need them the most. To help to address this challenge, we propose to establish a common decision support system for humanitarian operations - OSCAR.

We work to establish an integrated information platform...

...containing baseline & situational data from various sources...

...with certain analytical capabilities...

...to create a near real-time common operational picture & provide decision support...

for preparedness, response & recovery
